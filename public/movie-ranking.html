Attack on Tiatan

It is my first anime. I like it because of the storyline, foreshadowing, and of course for the action element

Demon Slayer

I like it because of the simplicity. You are a human and there are demons, so you must've figured out the stuff already

Another

One of the best horror anime i have watched, the mystery element is insane, you will be hooked up till the end

Maid Sama!

One of the best romance anime i have watched, very underrated. Storyline is amazing

Oregairu

If you are looking for both slice-of-life and romance, then Oregairy is the best choice for you trust me you wont't regret watching it
